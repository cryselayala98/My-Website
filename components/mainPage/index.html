About me

Hi, I’m Crisel Ayala, a Backend Developer with expertise in Java and Spring. I graduated with a bachelor’s degree in Systems Engineering from Francisco de Paula Santander University in Colombia.

I specialize in building scalable, efficient systems and integrating them seamlessly with frontend solutions. While backend development is my primary focus, I’m also skilled in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, enabling me to bridge the gap between backend and frontend effectively.

I’m passionate about creating user-centric, functional, and visually appealing applications. Beyond coding, I enjoy learning new technologies, exploring design systems, and mastering languages like English and French. Let’s work together to bring innovative ideas to life!
